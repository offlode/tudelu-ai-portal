How It Works

  1. 1 Sign in with your Tudelü email below.
  2. 2 Email your AI at chaim.fischer@tudelu.com — just write what you need in plain English. The AI monitors this inbox and responds to every team member.
  3. 3 Get a response — the AI will reply in the same email thread with your answer, status update, or next steps.
  4. 4 Give it orders — ask it to follow up with a customer, draft an email, remind you about a deal, or log a CRM interaction. It works for you. New
